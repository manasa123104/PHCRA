Primary Healthcare Center for Remote Access(PHCRA)

ABOUT THIS WEBSITE

Primary Healthcare Centre for Remote Access (PHCRA) is an application used by the call-centre employees when they receive a call on the toll-free number from any remote area citizens.
Using the Aadhar number provided by the patient, the application lists the personal and medical records of the patient for the use of call centre executive.
It helps the execute to give the appropriate advise depending on the requirement of the patient on-call and direct either to a nearby diagnostic centre or hospital.
The call centre execute will also fix an appointment for the patient on-call.
The hospitals and diagnostics will have read/update access to the medical records.
FEATURES OF THIS WEBSITE
1-Patient Basic Information Table-1 will contain the basic information of the patients.
Patient Hospitalization / Diagnostics Record Table-2 will store the detailed medical records of the patients.
